Camel

Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months!

  • Scientific Name: Camelus
  • Average Weight: 500kg
  • Average Life Span: 40 years
  • Habitat: Desert

People have kept camels for more than 4,000 years and still depend on them for survival all over the world.

The word ‘camel’ comes from an Arabic word: ǧml. This word is literally translated to beauty. Don’t ever try and say that camels are ugly!

There are 2 types of camel: Dromedary, which has one hump, and Bactrian, which has two humps.
